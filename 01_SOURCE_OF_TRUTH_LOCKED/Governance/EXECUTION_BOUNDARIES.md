# EXECUTION_BOUNDARIES

## Boundary Statements
1. Governance authority lives in Source of Truth documents and locked governance artifacts.
2. Spaces execute product work; they do not redefine enterprise root policy.
3. Apps and Packages are implementation layers, not policy authorities.
4. Approvals and Oversight paths are required for high-impact changes.

## Prohibited Actions
- Creating new top-level roots under `E:\` for official operations.
- Treating stale mirrors as canonical.
- Deleting legacy content without recovery checkpoint.

## Required Logging for Any Move/Merge
- UTC timestamp
- source path
- destination path
- classification (KEEP/MERGE/REDIRECT/ARCHIVE/DEPRECATE)
- approver identity
